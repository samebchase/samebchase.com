Eight semesters later: Sanity intact

I have passed four years of university. That's four years of coursework and eight semester examinations. I think that's a considerable acheivement. (self-back-pat)

It has been an overall positive experience. The only negative is that I could not study harder. However, I've learned many things about the world, about people and most importantly, myself.

The rest of my life is ahead, waiting to be lived out.
